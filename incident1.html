Headline

Deputy Chief Minister Nitin Patel held a meeting with Roads and Buildings department officials on Thursday evening to review road conditions in Gujarat. Out of an almost 1 lakh km network, over 10,000 km have been affected by rains in the state. Engineers will be sent to the field to review damage and ensure repair work is done quickly. According to a detailed report prepared by engineers of R&B department,the damage is estimated to be of about Rs 400 crore and over 10,000 km.Patel held a meeting with the chief of NHAI, secretary of Roads andBuilding department and senior engineers to understand the situation.According to a media statement, including national highways, statehighways and Panchayat-built roads, there is a road network of over 1lakh km in the state.
